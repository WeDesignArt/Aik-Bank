About Aik

The Aik digital app is the first Islamic Digital Banking app that allows its users to open an Asaan Digital Account (ADA) without any hassle. To open an account, all a customer needs to do is to download Aik app from Apple App store /Google Play store and follow simple steps. Aik app is designed to send and receive money to and from Aik account or any other domestic bank account which provide users a seamless experience of funds transfers. Moreover, it also provides features including Bill Payments and Mobile Load. Aik users can also order a Mastercard debit card for ATM withdrawals, POS purchase and online shopping.

Aik Digital has the following modes integrated in the app:

Inter Bank Funds Transfer (IBFT)

IBFT is a funds transfer mode provided by 1Link integrated in the Aik app. Aik users can transfer funds instantly to any bank on their account number or IBAN (International Bank Account Number). All the domestic banks in Pakistan are integrated with 1Link, funds transferred or received through IBFT are routed through 1Link to the recipient or sender bank.

A2A (Aik to Aik)

This mode of funds transfer is for Aik Digital users, this allows Aik users to send and receive money to and from Aik users.

RAAST

Raast is Pakistan’s first instant payment system that will enable end-to-end digital payments among individuals, businesses, and government entities instantaneously. Raast provides cheap and universal access to all players in the financial industry including commercial banks, microfinance banks, government entities and FinTech. this makes it a seamless for user and does not have any limit restriction on the amount transfer.
