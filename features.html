Master Card

Aik Classic Debit Mastercard is a dual interface enabled card; supporting NFC (contactless) and EMV/Chip technology, thus ensuring the utmost security of your debit card along with the convenience of tap and pay facility. With NFC feature on this card, you can just tap your card at NFC enabled ATMs or POS terminals displaying NFC sign to perform contactless transactions.
